📖 How to Play Harmonies

🎯 Your turn

  1. Click a token, then click a hex to place it. Do this 3 times (all from one space — the other two spaces are discarded after your first pick). The next token auto-selects; use ← / → to switch which one.
  2. Optionally take 1 animal card per turn (max 4 in hand), or discard & replace one.
  3. Place an animal cube when its habitat pattern is built on your board (patterns match in any rotation or mirror).
  4. Your turn ends automatically once all 3 tokens are placed — new tokens are drawn for the next turn.

Your board auto-saves — close the tab and come back anytime.

⭐ Scoring

  • 🌲 Trees — bush 1, tree 3, tall tree (2 brown+green) 7
  • ⛰️ Mountains — gray height 1/2/3 = 1/3/7, but 0 if not next to another mountain
  • 🌼 Fields — 5 per connected group of 2+ (a lone yellow = 0)
  • 🏠 Buildings — a stacked red scores 5 if next to 3+ different colors (a lone red = 0)
  • 💧 Water — longest river of 2/3/4/5/6 = 2/5/8/11/15 (+4 each beyond 6)
  • 🐾 Animals — the more cubes you place, the more points

Total points earn ☀️ suns: 40+ =1, 70+ =2, 90+ =3, 110+ =4, 130+ =5, 140+ =6, 150+ =7, 160+ =8.

🏁 Game ends

When the pouch has fewer than 9 tokens, or 2 or fewer empty hexes remain. Aim for the most suns!
