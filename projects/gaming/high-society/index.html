How to Play

Bid for luxury cards using your hand of money cards. The player with least money at game end is eliminated — then highest score wins.

Card Types

🏰
Luxury (10 cards)

Values 1–10. Add to your score. You want these.

👑
Prestige (3 cards)

Each one doubles your score. Incredibly powerful.

💀
Disgrace (3 cards)

Thief — discard a luxury you own
Scandale — halve your score
Passée — −5 points

Money Cards

You start with 11 money cards: 1 · 2 · 3 · 4 · 6 · 8 · 10 · 12 · 15 · 20 · 25

Money spent on wins is permanently gone. Folded money returns to your hand.

Game End

The game ends when the 4th red card (prestige or disgrace) is revealed — that card is not auctioned.

🏰 Luxury / Prestige Auction

You want this card.

  1. On your turn: raise (add money cards, total must beat current high) or fold.
  2. Cards on the table cannot be taken back — your bid grows each round.
  3. You can fold without adding new cards — never forced to raise.
  4. Last player standing wins the card. Their bid money is gone. Everyone else gets their money back.
Example: Alice bids 8, Bob bids 10, Alice raises to 14 (+6), Bob folds (gets 10 back). Alice wins; loses 8+6.

💀 Disgrace Auction

You don't want this card — it's a game of chicken.

  1. On your turn: bid (pressure others) or pass (take the card).
  2. The first player to pass takes the disgrace card and gets all their money back — no financial loss.
  3. All players who bid lose their bid money permanently.
Example: Alice bids 6, Bob bids 8, Charlie passes. Charlie takes Scandale (no money loss). Alice loses 6, Bob loses 8.

Step 1 — Eliminate the poorest

Count each player's remaining money. The player with the least money is eliminated — they cannot win regardless of their cards.

Tiebreaker: earlier joiner is eliminated.

Step 2 — Calculate scores

Score = (sum of luxury values) × 2prestige count
− 5 per Passée · ÷ 2 if Scandale
Luxuries 3+7+10, 0 prestige= 20
Same + 1 prestige= 40
Same + 2 prestige= 80
80 + Scandale= 40
80 + Passée= 75

Step 3 — Winner

Highest score wins. Tiebreaker: most money remaining.

  • 👑
    Prestige cards are everything. 3 prestige cards turns 20 into 160. Fight hard for them.
  • 💸
    Don't hoard money. You just can't run out — the goal is highest score, not most cash.
  • 🎭
    Disgrace bluffs work. Bid high in disgrace auctions to make others pass before you.
  • 💀
    Scandale is brutal with prestige. Halving 160 = 80. Force it on whoever has the most prestige.
  • 👀
    Watch opponents' money. A player with little money must fold soon — they can't keep bidding.
  • 🃏
    Thief with no luxuries. The next luxury you win is immediately discarded — plan around this.
